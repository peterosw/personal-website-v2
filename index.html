About Me

With over 25 years of experience as a Program Manager, I specialize in bridging the gap between technology and business to drive innovative solutions. My expertise spans cloud transformation, agile methodologies, and strategic program delivery, with a particular focus on creating secure, scalable, and user-centric solutions.

I'm passionate about fostering collaboration, driving efficiency through technology, and delivering measurable business outcomes while maintaining the highest standards of security and compliance.
